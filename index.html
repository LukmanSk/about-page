About Us

London based duo Suman Gurung and Celine Kreis founded Rue-L with an aim to create tairlored jersey pieces that give off the spirit of the street. Suman's Nepalese heritage and Celine's Swiss and Singaporean background bring strength and elegance to our garments. We take pride in the "how" of making clothes that last longer than your last relationship. We coined the mantra "Made To Wear to represent the antithesis to fast fashion. It's clothes for the long run, short sprint, for the bright n early and the all-nighters. Made to wear. Made with care. We made it for you.
